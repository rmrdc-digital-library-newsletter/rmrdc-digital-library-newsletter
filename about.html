About RMRDC CAS

About the CAS / SDI Platform

RMRDC Current Awareness Service (CAS) keeps RMRDC Library users informed about recent research outputs, policy briefs, technical reports, raw materials profiles, value-chain opportunities and industrial development knowledge.

The platform supports Current Awareness Service and Selective Dissemination of Information by matching users with resources related to their research interests.
